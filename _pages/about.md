I'm currently a postdotoral fellow in School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. My research interests include multimedia steganography, steganalysis, information hiding, etc.

Backgrounds
======
- 2025.05-Present: Postdoc, School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
- 2020.09-2025.06: Ph.D., School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
- 2016.09-2020.06: B.S., School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Beijing Jiaotong University.
